Choosing the Right Model

Selecting the appropriate model depends on your specific needs:

  • For general conversations: Start with Munet 2.0
  • For complex reasoning: Use Munet 2.0 Thinking
  • For quick responses: Choose Instant variants
  • For basic coding: Try Munet-Code 2.0
  • For professional development: Use Munet-Code 2.1 Pro
  • For the ultimate coding experience: Select Munet-Code 2.1 Pro Higher

Model Availability

Most models are currently in development and will be available when Munetios AI launches. Models marked as "Coming Soon" will be released in future updates. Stay tuned for announcements about model availability and new releases!
